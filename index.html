Leads Come In From Everywhere. Follow-Up Needs To Be Fast.

Estate agents deal with website forms, email enquiries, Facebook leads, viewing requests, valuation enquiries and landlord leads. When things get busy, valuable enquiries can slip through the cracks.

Missed Enquiries

Leads can get buried in inboxes, spreadsheets, forms and different platforms.

Slow Follow-Up

If a potential seller or landlord waits too long, they may contact another agency.

No Clear Tracking

Without a simple system, it is hard to see who has been contacted and who still needs action.

How LeadFlow Systems Works

A simple demo automation system that captures the enquiry, organises it, alerts your team and helps prepare a follow-up faster.

1

Lead Comes In

A buyer, seller, tenant or landlord submits an enquiry through a form, email enquiry, website form, Facebook lead or manual entry.

2

Lead Is Logged Automatically

The enquiry is added into a simple CRM sheet with name, contact details, enquiry type, message and status.

3

Your Team Is Notified

An instant email notification is sent so your team can respond quickly.

4

Follow-Up Is Prepared

A ready-to-review email draft can be created so your agency can respond faster while staying in control of what gets sent.
